Corrugated / wrap pipe

The forming is different; the need for steady weight and steady dosing is not.

Corrugated and wrap pipe form differently from conventional pipe, but the demand at the extruder is the same: weight per metre has to stay steady, masterbatch and additive ratios have to stay accurate, and resin shouldn't be over-consumed.

Sealion's gravimetric and masterbatch control sit at the extruder feed throat, driving output and ratio from the recipe. The configuration for your machine and process is confirmed by an engineer on site.
