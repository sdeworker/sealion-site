Plastic piping

From weighing at the feed throat to quality-gated storage — measurement and control across the whole extrusion process.

The extrusion process and quality control of plastic pipe, film and cable is the application range of Sealion's gravimetric control system.

A full line can carry: gravimetric and masterbatch control at the feed throat, ultrasonic thickness gauging at the water tank, quality-gated storage or intelligent inspection at the line end, with the data flowing into cloud remote monitoring — from the scale to the cloud, one line covered end to end.
