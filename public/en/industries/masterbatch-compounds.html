Masterbatch & compounds

Masterbatch has long been dosed by hand — inaccurate components, uneven mixing, and a moment's lapse that can cost more than it should.

Every product has a different formulation, and masterbatch has long been mixed by hand — inaccurate dosing and uneven mixing, leaving pipe quality unstable and adding labour cost and needless effort. And because it rests on an operator's diligence, a moment's carelessness can bring a significant, irredeemable loss.

Where it applies: plastic film, cable, pipe, plastic modification, chemicals, and any industry needing several components dosed to an exact ratio. Dosing accuracy ±1%, or 4‰ with masterbatch weighing.
