PE water pipe

Extruded in a single pass from dedicated polyethylene for municipal water networks, irrigation and agricultural sprinkler systems — acid-, alkali- and corrosion-resistant, and every metre has to hold.

PE water pipe is extruded in a single pass from dedicated polyethylene, and goes into municipal water networks, irrigation projects and agricultural sprinkler systems — plastic pipe suited to acid, alkali and corrosive environments.

On the line, Sealion's gravimetric control and ultrasonic online thickness systems control the product dynamically as it runs, so the quality of every metre holds.
