PE gas pipe

Natural gas is flammable and explosive; a cracked or leaking pipe is unthinkable — which is why gas pipe demands the tightest wall thickness of all.

Natural gas is flammable and explosive: if a pipe cracks and gas leaks, the consequences are unthinkable. That is why wall thickness matters more here than anywhere else.

Sealion's gravimetric control and ultrasonic online thickness systems solve uneven wall thickness on gas-pipe lines — weight control steadies the output while ultrasound watches wall, eccentricity and ovality as the line runs.

Sealion is an invited member of the China Gas Association's Science & Technology Committee.
