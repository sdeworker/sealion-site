Medical catheter

Small, complex, geometrically exacting and hygienically strict — and above all, it needs the wall thickness to hold.

Medical catheters are small and precise, complex in shape, exacting in geometry and strict on hygiene — and above all they need steady wall-thickness control.

In extrusion, Sealion's gravimetric system keeps the material output steady while the ultrasonic gauge continuously measures and records wall, inner and outer diameter to meet the process requirement. The SL-Ultrascan series exists for exactly this kind of small bore: wall 0.18–10mm, diameter 2–50mm, with an original auto-adjusting structure and a probe that centres and follows on-line.
