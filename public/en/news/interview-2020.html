Modern Piping interviews Sealion general manager Mr. Luo Xiyang

2020-08

As part of its “Brand Interview” series, Modern Piping’s media centre visited member companies together with the CAWA modern piping committee secretariat.

On 24 July the writer met Mr. Luo Xiyang, general manager of Guangzhou Sea-lion Software Technology, to hear how gravimetric control and online inspection safeguard product quality in the piping industry.
