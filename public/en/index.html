Service & support

Getting the system online is where we start

A measurement & control system isn’t a box you buy and shelve — it has to grow into your machine, die and formulation. Sealion’s service starts the day we arrive at your plant.

S1

On-site installation & commissioning

Our engineers handle mechanical installation, electrical wiring and full system commissioning on site, calibrating to your machine, die and formulation until the line runs steady weight-per-metre and wall thickness.

S2

Operator & maintenance training

Hands-on training for line leaders and equipment staff: daily operation, what each parameter means, and how to read and clear common alarms — so the system lands in capable hands.

S3

Spare parts & consumables

Long-term supply of key spares — load cells, ultrasonic probes, control modules — so even older systems stay serviceable and a discontinued part never stops your line.

S4

Remote diagnostics & updates

Lines connected to cloud monitoring let us pull running data remotely, pinpoint faults, and push parameter and firmware updates — most issues without waiting for an engineer to arrive.
