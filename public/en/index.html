About Sealion

An industrial-control company for extrusion

Founded in 2008, Guangzhou Sealion Software Technology Co., Ltd. is a national high-tech enterprise integrating R&D, manufacturing and sales of industrial control systems. Our work spans hardware EDA, low-level DSP, ARM and host software, electrical-control design, PLC programming and mechanical design — from core algorithms to full-line solutions.

Products

Measurement & control for extrusion

From inlet weighing to inbound QC, from single-machine control to the extrusion cloud — eight core systems across the plastic-extrusion line.

01

Gravimetric Control System

Weight-per-meter

Installed at the extruder inlet, PID closed-loop control keeps weight-per-meter precise in real time — stabilising quality and saving 2%–5% material.

Learn more →
02

Gravimetric Masterbatch Control System

Masterbatch

Controls masterbatch dosing while controlling weight-per-meter; the masterbatch unit tracks the host dynamically at ±1% ratio accuracy.

Learn more →
03

Gravimetric Masterbatch Weighting System

Weighing

Masterbatch metered by loss-in-weight, each component controlled independently, recipes archived and traceable — 1 to 3+ components.

Learn more →
04

Ultrasonic Thickness Online (Big Pipe)

Thickness · Big

Non-contact ultrasonic measurement of wall thickness, eccentricity, ovality and OD at 0.01mm resolution, for Φ63–1200 large pipe.

Learn more →
05

Ultrasonic Thickness Online (Small Tube)

Thickness · Small

On-line thickness for small tubes with an original auto-diameter structure and self-centring probe — wall 0.18–10mm, dia 2–50mm.

Learn more →
06

Pipe Inbound Quality & Safety System

Inbound QC

Full inspection at the line end: laser thickness, encoder OD, ovality, weight, length; qualified pipe turned to storage, data to MES.

Learn more →
07

Online Intelligent Pipe Inspection System

Inspection

Automatic inspection platform at the line end — laser thickness, OD, ovality and turning in one, data uploaded for remote quality control.

Learn more →
08

Cloud-Based Extrusion Monitoring System

Cloud

Links ERP and line execution systems, streams production data to the cloud — equipment, orders, curves, alarms and output all visible remotely.

Learn more →

Service & support

Getting the system online is where we start

A measurement & control system isn’t a box you buy and shelve — it has to grow into your machine, die and formulation. Sealion’s service starts the day we arrive at your plant.

From explaining the product before the sale, to guiding it during, to training people to use it after — every step is meant to let the customer see what the product is worth.

Dozensof service engineers, working across the country
One calland the team responds to your problem
On siteinstallation and operator training, until your people really own the process
S1

On-site installation & commissioning

Our engineers handle mechanical installation, electrical wiring and full system commissioning on site, calibrating to your machine, die and formulation until the line runs steady weight-per-metre and wall thickness.

S2

Operator & maintenance training

Hands-on training for line leaders and equipment staff: daily operation, what each parameter means, and how to read and clear common alarms — so the system lands in capable hands.

S3

Spare parts & consumables

Long-term supply of key spares — load cells, ultrasonic probes, control modules — so even older systems stay serviceable and a discontinued part never stops your line.

S4

Remote diagnostics & updates

Lines connected to cloud monitoring let us pull running data remotely, pinpoint faults, and push parameter and firmware updates — most issues without waiting for an engineer to arrive.
