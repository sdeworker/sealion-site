Cloud-Based Extrusion Monitoring System

Connecting ERP with the line, lifting the shop floor to the cloud

Read this chapter as pages Download PDF

In this chapter

  2. Functional characteristics

Overview#

With the global competition of manufacturing industry, enterprises are facing various challenges and changes. Industrial upgrading, energy saving and emission reduction, management efficiency, production cost, product quality and order delivery time are all powerful links for enterprises to maintain their competitiveness.

Extrusion cloud remote monitoring system is dedicated to enterprise intelligent manufacturing, which is specially for online measurement and control and remote monitoring of extrusion industry production line. The monitoring system can connect the company's ERP system and the execution system of the production line. Through real-time collection of production site data, and transmission to the cloud server or internal network server, it solves the management's visualization and whole process monitoring of the production process. The production data of each link is transmitted to the remote monitoring system in real time, and the unqualified data in the production process is timely corrected. Thus, the production response time is shortened, the product quality is improved, the production cost is reduced, and the core competitiveness of the enterprise can be improved.
