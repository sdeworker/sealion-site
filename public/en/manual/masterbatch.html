What is it#

The Gravimetric Masterbatch Control System can control the masterbatch adding while controlling the pipe meter weight — this system makes gravimetric control and masterbatch control together.

Every product is made of different components. Masterbatch adding has been mixed by workers for a long time; this can make inaccurate components and uneven mixture, leading to pipe quality instability and increasing labour cost and intensity. A worker's carelessness may bring significant irredeemable loss.

Application field#

Plastic film, cable, pipe, plastic modification, chemical industry and multi-component adding industry.

Advantages#

  • Masterbatch machine dynamic change along with the host, control the proportion at any time strictly
  • PID closed-loop control, intelligent, high precision
  • Mixed continuously on-line, save mixing time, improve efficiency
  • DC servo motor without maintenance
  • Mixed automatically, keep the mixture even
  • Control by bottom software and hardware makes operation more reliable
