Company profile#

Founded in 2008, Guangzhou Sea-lion Software Technology Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise with technological innovation at its core. It specializes in the R&D, production and sales of intelligent measurement and control systems for industrial extrusion production, and is committed to promoting the digital and intelligent upgrading of the manufacturing industry.

The company has a sound technical system, covering hardware EDA design, DSP R&D, software development and electrical control system design, and possesses a full-chain R&D capability from core algorithms to complete machine applications. Its core products include weight-per-meter control systems, ultrasonic online thickness measurement systems, weight-per-meter color masterbatch control systems, precision color masterbatch machines, pipe quality and safety warehousing systems, and pipe online intelligent detection systems, providing efficient, accurate, visual and digital factory solutions for the plastic pipe and cable industry.

Sea-lion Technology has obtained more than 60 national patents and software copyrights, and has been awarded many honors such as “High-Tech Enterprise”, “Specialized, Refined, Characteristic and Novel Enterprise”, “Technology Little Giant Enterprise” and “Gazelle Enterprise”, demonstrating the company's outstanding technical strength and industry influence.

Adhering to the spirit of “Professionalism, Concentration, Dedication and Sincerity”, Sea-lion Technology continues to use intelligent technologies and innovative solutions to help customers reduce costs and increase efficiency, and jointly build efficient, green and smart future factories.
